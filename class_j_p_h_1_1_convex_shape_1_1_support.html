virtual float JPH::ConvexShape::Support::GetConvexRadius ( ) const
pure virtual

Convex radius of shape. Collision detection on penetrating shapes is much more expensive, so you can add a radius around objects to increase the shape. This makes it far less likely that they will actually penetrate.

Implemented in JPH::BoxShape::Box, JPH::CapsuleShape::CapsuleNoConvex, JPH::CapsuleShape::CapsuleWithConvex, JPH::ConvexHullShape::HullNoConvex, JPH::ConvexHullShape::HullWithConvex, JPH::ConvexHullShape::HullWithConvexScaled, JPH::CylinderShape::Cylinder, JPH::SphereShape::SphereNoConvex, JPH::SphereShape::SphereWithConvex, JPH::TaperedCapsuleShape::TaperedCapsule, JPH::TriangleShape::TriangleNoConvex, and JPH::TriangleShape::TriangleWithConvex.
